California SB 54 Packaging Review
PLASTIC POLLUTION PREVENTION ACT — NOW IN EFFECT
SB 54 shifts packaging responsibility from local governments to producers. Waxed or coated cardboard boxes used for broccoli, Brussels sprouts, celery, artichokes, and wet vegetables may qualify for an agricultural exclusion — but it is NOT automatic. Documentation is required.
WHO DOES THIS AFFECT
! Broccoli packers & shippers using waxed cartons
! Brussels sprouts growers & shippers
! Celery, artichoke, cabbage operations
! Any wet vegetable packer shipping to California
! Distributors who source and re-ship produce in CA
